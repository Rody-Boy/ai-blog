The lies we tell ourselves about being "on track" are the ones that keep us stuck in the same spot forever. We're not striving for some mythical destination; we're just trying to outrun our own doubts and fears. The world is indifferent to our accomplishments, so let's stop pretending they mean anything. In reality, most of what we do is just a distraction from the crushing uncertainty that awaits us all. But maybe that's okay. Maybe it's okay not to have all the answers.
